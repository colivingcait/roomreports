FAQ

Questions we hear a lot.

HostHow do residents submit without creating an account?

Every org gets three universal links: Move-In, Self-Check, Report Maintenance. You share the link or print a QR (we include a flyer generator). Residents type their street name, pick their place, and submit — no signup, no password.

HostCan I change the default inspection checklists?

Yes. Every inspection type has a template you own. Add, edit, remove, and drag to reorder items from the Templates section. Your changes apply to future inspections only — completed ones stay exactly as submitted.

ResidentDo I need the app?

No. Scan the QR on your wall, enter your street name, and submit. It works in any phone browser.

TeammateWhat do cleaners and handypeople see?

Only the properties they're assigned to, and only the tickets / inspections relevant to them. Hosts manage assignments from the Team section.

HostIs there a paper trail for lease violations?

Yes. Flagged items can be marked as maintenance, lease violation, or both. Violations get their own log where you can record follow-up actions — text, email, verbal, written notice — with timestamps.
